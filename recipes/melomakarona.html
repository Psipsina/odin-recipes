Melomakarona recipe

Description

Another traditional recipe for Christmas cookies from Greece with honey and cinamon flavours !

Ingredients

For the syrup

  • 500g water
  • 800g granulated sugar
  • 150g honey
  • 3 sticks of cinamon
  • 3 whole cloves
  • 1 orange cut in half

1st mixture

  • 400g orange juice
  • 400g seed oil
  • 180g olive oil
  • 50g icing sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2-3 teaspoons cinamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • orange zest of 2 oranges

2nd mixture

  • 1 kilo all purpose flour
  • 200g semolina, fine

Steps

For the syrup

  1. Boil all of the ingredients for the syrup, apart from the honey, until the sugar melts. Remove from heat. Add the honey and mix till combined
  2. Let the syrup cool for 3-4 hours. It must be cold by the time the cookies come out from the oven.
  3. You can prepare the syrup from the day before.

For the cookies

  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C set to fan
  2. To make the cookies, you need to prepare 2 separate mixtures.
  3. For the first mixture, add all of the ingredients in a large bowl. Mix, using a hand whisk.
  4. In a separate bowl, add all of the ingredients for the second mixture.
  5. Combine the first and second mixture.
  6. Mix by hand, very gently and for a very short time (10 seconds at the most). If you mix longer the mixture will split or curdle.
  7. Mold cookie dough into oval shapes, 3-4 cm in length, 30 g each. Try to keep them as similar as possible.
  8. Bake for about 20-25 minutes, until they are crunchy and golden brown.
  9. As soon as you remove them from the oven, soak the hot cookies in the syrup for 10 seconds.
  10. Allow them to drain on a wire rack.
  11. Drizzle with honey and chopped walnuts.
